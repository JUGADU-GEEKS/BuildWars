About Us

College Trade Bazaar is a platform created by students, for students. We understand the high costs of educational materials and lab equipment, so we've built this marketplace to help students buy and sell used items at affordable prices.

Our mission is to promote sustainability and affordability in education by giving laboratory equipment and educational materials a second life.

Sustainability

Reducing waste by reusing quality equipment

Affordability

Making education more accessible through reasonable prices

Community

Building connections between students across departments
